The Last Residents of Schowe - 1944
The following list is excerpted from Heimatbuch der Gemeinde Schowe - 1961


The text of the list is presented here in the original German version in order to preserve the information in as authentic a manner as possible. Each entry is prefixed with the street address. Alt-Schowe did not have assigned house numbers, in this list the street name is prefixed with the number corresponding to the number shown on the map of Schowe - These numbers correspond to the the order in which the listings appear in the Heimatbuch. The prefixed address is included in order to make searching this list easier.
